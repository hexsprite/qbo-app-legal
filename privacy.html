Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20

Data We Access

The App accesses your QuickBooks Online data (transactions, accounts, balances) via the Intuit API using OAuth tokens you authorize.

Data We Store

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user's device. We do not transmit your QuickBooks data to any third party or external server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Retention

Revoke access anytime in QuickBooks Online → Apps. Local tokens are deleted on revocation or uninstall.

Security

Tokens are stored using OS-level secure storage. All API calls use HTTPS.
